## What's New

### Modern Tech Stack

The blog now runs on **Next.js 15** with **TypeScript** and **Tailwind CSS**. Instead of Hugo's templating system, we have a React-based design system built with glassmorphism, bioluminescent accents, and the deep-sea aesthetic from projects like visual-toolkit and creative-playground.

### LLM-Friendly Workflows

One of the goals was to make post creation seamless from an AI interface. Posts are simple Markdown files with YAML frontmatter:

```yaml
---
title: "Your Title"
date: "2026-01-20"
excerpt: "Brief summary"
tags: ["tag1", "tag2"]
author: "Veronica Ray"
featured: false
---
```

Just write Markdown, commit to git, and the site auto-deploys via GitHub Actions.

### Design Philosophy

The visual aesthetic draws from my creative work:

- **Dark foundations**: Very dark blues and grays (#0d0d12, #0f0f12)
- **Bioluminescent accents**: Cyan glows that feel organic and deep-sea
- **Glassmorphism**: Blurred backgrounds with subtle borders
- **Monospace typography**: JetBrains Mono with generous letter-spacing
- **Slow animations**: Glacial timing for a premium, organic feel
